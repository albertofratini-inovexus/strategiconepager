Founders Club — 2027 vision
The target model
Umbrella model · Hyper-scalable · Inbound-first · Tested summer 2026
🏛
Founders Club umbrella
Single model combining accompaniment and sourcing. The accelerator becomes one pillar of the Club — not the reverse. Club access = standalone value.
📡
Inbound-first 80% target
Shift from 100% outbound diagnostic to cohort + marketing model. 80% inbound sourcing in medium term — powered by community gravity and Club brand.
🔁
Cohort model co-existing with agnostic track
Scalable cohort format alongside the current individualized model. Both tracks co-exist — volume at scale without losing tailored, founder-centric support.
🤝
Community as the core product
Deal Club mentor-investors become the backbone of a proprietary founder network. Relationships outlast the program and create compounding value.
⚙️
Automating already-structured support
Mentoring, KPIs, workshops, perks — already structured. Priority: automate via Affinity + platform. Business intros = the one remaining pillar to formalize.
💰
Investment model unchanged
Tickets €100k–€300k. Selection criteria preserved. The Club amplifies dealflow — it doesn't replace the investment thesis or evaluation logic.
🔬
Build → Test → Scale
Prototype early May 2026. Pilot summer 2026 with select portfolio. Validated model end 2026. Full scaling 2027.

Open questions for the upcoming brainstorming session
Strategic questions to align on — senior team
These are suggested topics to frame the conversation — not a fixed agenda. The brainstorming session will be completely open and unconstrained. Capture everything, judge nothing.
01
What role does corporate finance play within the Founders Club?
How do we best showcase our fundraising support as a core pillar of the Club? Think: helping founders prepare and access their next rounds — intros to co-investors, fundraising readiness, deal structuring support.
02
How do the cohort and agnostic models co-exist operationally?
Same team? Same platform? Different pricing tiers? When does the agnostic model remain the default, and when does the cohort track apply?
03
What is the 80% inbound acquisition engine — concretely?
Which events, content formats, or community activities drive qualified inbound? What's the funnel from Club member to accelerated startup?
04
Which support workflows get automated first — and how?
Mentor matching, KPI tracking, perk activation? What's the Affinity + platform stack that removes manual effort without losing the human touch?
05
How do we finally structure business introductions?
Ticketing, response SLA, Affinity tracking? Who owns intro requests — the success manager, lead mentor, or a new dedicated role?
06
Should we invest in learning expeditions abroad — or is the cost/effort ratio a dealbreaker?
Given the potential price point and operational workload involved, is it worth building this internally — or should we explore partnerships with existing players to deliver international exposure without the full cost?
